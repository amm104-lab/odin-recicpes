German Pancackes


Description

German pancackes are wider and rather flat compared to american pancackes. Furthermore they are in general not eaten for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Flour: 250g
  • Milk: 500ml
  • Eggs: 4
  • Sugar: 50g
  • Salt: 1 pinch

Steps

  1. Add dry ingredients together
  2. Add milk during mixing
  3. Add eggs
  4. Mix again
  5. Let it rest for up to half an hour
